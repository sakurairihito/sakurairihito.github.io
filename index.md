I am Rihito Sakurai, a researcher working in the field of quantum technology.
My current interests include tensor networks, quantum computations, and their applications to practical usages such as material science, finance and so on.
I enjoy exploring new ideas across disciplines and collaborations with others fields to push the boundaries of what quantum technologies can achieve.
